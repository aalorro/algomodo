Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Algomodo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a generative art application available on Android and the web. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Algomodo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not serve advertisements.

Camera and Photo Access

The app may request access to your device camera or photo gallery solely for use with image processing generators. Images are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Data Storage

All data — including presets, settings, and generated artwork — is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

Algomodo does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. The app may use the device's share functionality to export artwork, which is handled entirely by your operating system.

Children's Privacy

Algomodo does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at artmondo@hybols.com.
